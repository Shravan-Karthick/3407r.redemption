About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Team 3407R, Redemption, is a private VEX Robotics team composed of passionate and dedicated high school students. We are committed to achieving excellence in robotics by designing, building, and programming innovative robots to compete in the VEX Robotics Competition. Our mission is to develop critical skills in STEM, collaboration, and strategic thinking.
